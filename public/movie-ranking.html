Movie Ranking List according to Raja

My Top 3 Movies

I want to eat your pancreas

A Japanese animated film. It tells the story of a high school boy who is an introvert and finds a diary belonging to his classmate, who is suffering from a terminal illness. As they spend time together, they form a deep bond and learn to appreciate life.

3 idiots

A Bollywood comedy-drama film that follows three engineering students and their journey through college life, highlighting the importance of friendship and the pursuit of dreams over rote learning.

chichore

A film that teaches about the importance of struggle and resilience in life.
